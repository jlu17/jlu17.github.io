Context: About Fratty Bear

Fratty Bear is a custom clothing company focused primarily on servicing student organization and Greek life in different parts of California. Because most of their customer base is Greek life, they donate 10% of their proceeds to a nonprofit of their customer's choice. I was in charge of doing a basic redesign of their website to bring in more customers using React.
